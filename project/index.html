🍰 Chocolate Cake

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs

Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Add wet ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into a greased baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30–35 minutes. Let cool before serving.

Prep Time: 45 mins | Serves: 6
